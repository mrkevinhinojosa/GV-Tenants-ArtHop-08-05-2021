ArtHop is BACK in downtown Fresno. This Fresno Arts Council community event features art galleries, exhibits, museums, tasty food trucks, and street eats. We encourage you to visit Granville's own M Street Arts Complex to see original artwork from our resident artists. As always, this event is open to the public, and there is no charge for parking at the M Street Arts Complex after 6:00 p.m.
